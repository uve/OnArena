Leagues are a way for multiple people to post to the same stream.

To start posting to a league, go to its page, click Join, and type your message in the field that appears.

Leagues allow you to separate conversations, because messages posted to a league are not displayed on your personal stream. If you think your followers should follow the topic, tell them to join the league!

Want your own leagues?

To create your own leagues you'll need to Sign in or Sign up.
